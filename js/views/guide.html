Guide

A simple color dropper that captures the colors in the screen.

Sections

  • Current Color: shows the current color in the screen pointed by the mouse cursor.
  • Color Models: you can select 3 different models: Hex, RGB, HSL. Simply click on it, to change the model.
  • Captured Colors: contains all the colors saved.

Keyboard commands:

  • Hit SPACE to capture, and save a color.
  • To be super precise you can use your ARROW keys. It will move your mouse cursor 1px by 1px
